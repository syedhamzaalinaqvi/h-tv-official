Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add my own videos?

Currently, only administrators can add new videos to the platform. Contact us if you have content to share.

Is this content safe for all ages?

No. The Boys features extreme stunts that should not be attempted at home. Viewer discretion is advised.

Can I download videos?

We don't currently support video downloads as we embed content from other platforms.
